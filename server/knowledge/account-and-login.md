# Account & Login

To reset your password, go to the sign-in page and click "Forgot password". Enter
the email on your account and we'll send a reset link that's valid for one hour.
If the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check your spam folder and make
sure you used the address you signed up with.

You can change the email address on your account from Account -> Profile. We'll
send a confirmation link to the new address; the change only takes effect after
you click that link, so your old email stays active until then.

For extra security you can enable two-factor authentication (2FA) under Account ->
Security. Once enabled, you'll enter a code from your authenticator app each time
you sign in. If you lose access to your authenticator, use one of the backup
codes shown when you set up 2FA.

We never ask for your password by email or phone. If you receive a message asking
for it, treat it as phishing and report it to support.
