# Contacting Support

Our support team is available by live chat and email Monday through Friday, 9 AM
to 6 PM ET. We're closed on weekends and major US holidays. Messages sent outside
those hours are answered the next business day in the order received.

Live chat is the fastest option for time-sensitive issues like a payment problem
or a delivery that's going wrong. You'll find the chat launcher in the bottom-right
corner of any page while signed in.

For anything that isn't urgent, email support@example.com. Including your order
number and a short description of the problem helps us resolve it on the first
reply without back-and-forth.

We aim to respond to all email within one business day. Complex issues that need
investigation (for example a disputed charge) can take longer, and we'll keep you
updated while we look into it.
