# Refunds & Returns

You can request a refund within 30 days of the original purchase date. To start a
refund, sign in and go to Account -> Orders, find the order, and click "Request
refund". You'll get an email confirmation once the request is submitted.

Refunds are issued to your original payment method. After we approve a refund it
usually takes 5-10 business days for the money to appear on your statement; the
exact timing depends on your bank or card issuer.

To be eligible, items must be unused and returned in their original packaging.
Final-sale and clearance items cannot be returned or refunded. Shipping charges
are non-refundable unless the return is the result of our error (for example, a
wrong or defective item was sent).

If your order arrived damaged, do not return it through the normal flow. Instead
contact support within 7 days with a photo of the damage and we'll send a
replacement or issue a full refund, including shipping.
