# Shipping & Delivery

We offer three shipping speeds at checkout: Standard (3-5 business days), Express
(2 business days), and Overnight (next business day if ordered before 1 PM ET).
Orders placed on weekends or holidays begin processing the next business day.

Standard shipping is free on orders over $50. Below that, a flat $5 fee applies.
Express and Overnight are priced at checkout based on weight and destination.

Once your order ships you'll receive a tracking number by email. You can also
find it under Account -> Orders by selecting the order and viewing its status.
Tracking can take up to 24 hours to show movement after a label is created.

We currently ship within the United States and Canada only. We do not ship to PO
boxes for Express or Overnight orders. If a package is marked delivered but you
can't find it, wait 24 hours (carriers sometimes scan early) and check with
neighbors before contacting support.
