# Subscriptions & Billing

We offer three plans: Free, Pro, and Enterprise. Pro and Enterprise are billed
monthly by default, and you can switch to annual billing for a discount from
Account -> Billing. Your plan renews automatically at the end of each period.

To upgrade or downgrade, go to Account -> Billing and choose a new plan. Upgrades
take effect immediately and we prorate the charge for the rest of the current
period. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period, so you
keep paid features until then.

To cancel, go to Account -> Billing and click "Cancel plan". Your subscription
stays active until the end of the period you've already paid for; we do not issue
prorated refunds for the unused portion of a cancelled subscription.

If a payment fails, your subscription enters a "past due" state and we retry the
charge over the next few days. Update your card under Account -> Billing to resolve
it. If the retries don't succeed, the plan is downgraded to Free until payment is
fixed.
